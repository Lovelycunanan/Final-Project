Volleyball

My journey with volleyball began in grade school at Victor Mager School, where I first discovered my passion for the sport. It was there that I honed my skills, learned the intricacies of teamwork, and developed a love for the fast-paced nature of the game. As I progressed to Windsor Park Collegiate for high school, my commitment to volleyball deepened. Representing my high school team allowed me to compete at a more competitive level, where I continued to refine my techniques and embrace the camaraderie of the sport. Even after high school, I carried my love for volleyball into the realm of recreational play, participating in friendly matches for the sheer joy and camaraderie that the game brings. Beyond the structured environments of school competition, playing volleyball for fun has allowed me to sustain the connection to a sport that has been an integral part of my personal and athletic journey.

Singing

I discovered my love for singing at a young age, and it quickly became a passion that I've carried with me throughout my life. From volunteering events to school functions and even weddings, I have had the joy of sharing my voice in various settings. These experiences not only allowed me to express my musicality but also brought a sense of fulfillment as I contributed my talent to meaningful moments and celebrations. Singing has been a constant thread in the fabric of my life, weaving through diverse occasions and connecting me with others through the shared joy of music.

Guitar

My exploration of the guitar commenced as a casual interest that blossomed into a fulfilling musical endeavor. Starting with the basics, I dedicated time to learning chords and mastering the instrument's techniques. Playing the guitar quickly became a source of solace and self-expression, allowing me to convey emotions and tell stories through music. From strumming familiar tunes to experimenting with my compositions, each chord and melody has been a step in my musical evolution. The guitar, for me, is not just an instrument; it's a companion in my creative journey, offering a unique channel for personal expression and a constant source of joy.
